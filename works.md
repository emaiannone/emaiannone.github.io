**E. Iannone**, F. Pecorelli, D. Di Nucci, F. Palomba, A. De Lucia.  
In Proceedings of the 28th IEEE/ACM International Conference on Program Comprehension (ICPC 2020),
Seoul, South Korea, 2020.
